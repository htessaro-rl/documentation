You can find your Visual KPI license expiration date and additional information about your Visual KPI site on the Help screen of your site. Follow the steps below to find the detailed information:

1. In your Visual KPI site, click **Help** in the navigation menu.

2. At the bottom of the Site Status section you will find a link <SvgFont icon={251} /> **For detailed information about this Visual KPI site please click here**. Click it.

  imgURL="img/faqs/expiration-date-gif.gif"
  alt="navigating to site settings gif"
/>

Under the section **Site Settings**, you will find your license key details and additional information about your site. This is exemplified in the image below:
